Shipment

What is the shipping time

Standard Shipment The shipping time for your order is estimated to be 2-3 working days. Please keep in mind that this is an approximate timeframe, and actual delivery times may vary depending on factors such as your location, shipping method, and any potential delays that may occur during transit. If you have any specific concerns or need more precise information about your shipment, please don't hesitate to reach out to our customer service team for further assistance.

Expedited Shipment
By default, we use FedEx for most expedited orders. With the battery items, we would ship with DHL. Normally, the expedited shipping will take around 17 days. Please take a note that the shipping time is an estimated time not proper shipping time. For now, NONE of delivery companies offer their shipping time guarantee service. If your order is over 3000rs, we highly recommend to purchase the expedited shipping. It is more safe and guaranteed.

Still not receive?
"In our shipping experience, the longest delivery time for orders with free shipping within India typically extends to around 5-7 days. If your parcel hasn't reached you within this period after you receive the tracking information, please don't hesitate to get in touch with us. We're committed to resolving any issues you may encounter, which may include offering a refund or arranging for a reshipment. Please note that the 5-7 day window begins from the day you receive the tracking information, not from the day you initially place your order."

How do I purchase fast shipping

To purchase fast shipping, simply select the 'Fast Shipping' option during the checkout process when placing your order.

Pricing

Do I need to pay customs or import duties?

Yes. It depends on your local customs policy. You are responsible for paying customs or import duties that your country may assign to you.

Order Issue

How do I require for an order cancellation?

It happens when we purchase online. We are completely understand. Please leave mesasges via Live Chat or Messanger or discord with your order number and tell us you want to cancel the order. We will refund to you as soon as possible.

How do I require for a replacement when I receive the defective item or missing item?

Please share with us your order number and photos or videos about your issue. The photo should contains the item you received and the shipping label. They are required to be in one photo. The Photo is better to be HD photo.
